Networked and Communication Systems Research Lab (NEST)
Welcome!Our group conducts research spanning a broad range of topics in networked and communication systems, including wireless networks, sensor networks, and mobile computing. We focus on fundamental networking and communication problems. Our research methodology is a combination of analytical and empirical approaches. We have been actively collaborating with researchers from various places.
